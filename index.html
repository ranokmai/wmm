Where's My Money?!

Where's My Money is a new way to track your loans and transactions on Android.

Have you ever bought food for a friend, let them borrow your favorite game, or lent them some cash only to forget and remember months later? Where's My Money is a new application for Android that will help you keep track of your loans and IOUs, send reminders to your friends, and collect what's owed.

Track Your Money

Where's My Money makes it easy to keep track of your money.

  • Easily browse your transaction history using filters
  • Archive old transactions to keep them out of the way
  • Permanently delete private or sensitive transactions
  • Keep track of money or items that you owe to other people

Create Transactions

Easily create new transactions on the fly.

  • Create new monetary or item transactions
  • Link transactions with your Android contacts
  • Include a description or take a picture for your records
  • Assign due dates and reminders to transactions

Manage Your Contacts

Use Where's My Money to create transactions and IOUs for your contacts.

  • Complete Android contact integration
  • Browse your transaction history with each contact
  • View transaction statistics for your contacts

Analyze Transaction Stats

Where's My Money provides detailed transaction statistics.

  • View graphs of your recent transaction activity
  • Keep track of your largest borrowers
  • View historical graphs for the last few days, weeks, or months
